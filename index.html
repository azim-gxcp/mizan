Mizan

Does your financial transactions or agreements hold up to Islamic first principles?

A diagnostic for any financial arrangement - Islamic bank products, business trade, personal loans between family and friends, investments, currency exchange, and crypto, evaluated against these four foundational concepts. Not institutional Shariah compliance frameworks. Primary sources only.

Developed by M Azim Abdul Majeed

The four first principles
Riba (ربا) — the prohibited excess: any stipulated surplus above the principal, arising without genuine counter-value and without real risk.
Qard (قرض) — the loan: a piece cut from wealth, given freely. Only the exact equivalent (Mithl) returns. Any stipulated excess above that is Riba.
Dayn (دين) — the outstanding obligation: fixed at the point it arises. Any increase through delay is Riba al-Nasi'ah. Shares its root with Din (faith) and Dayyan (Allah the Judge).
Bay' (بيع) — the bilateral exchange: two things moving in two directions. Requires genuine prior ownership, genuine risk, and genuine counter-value (Iwad) on both sides. This is what Allah has permitted.
+ Seven Ribawi characteristics — Mithiliyyah, Qimah Dhatiyyah, Baqa', Rawaj, Sifah Ma'lumah, Kayl/Wazn, Hajah 'Ammah - determine which monetary medium these rules apply to in full.
Step 0 | Which monetary medium is involved?
Choose your analytical track. This is the most important choice in the diagnostic - it determines which framework is applied to every finding. There are two analytical tracks and three entry points.
Full Ribawi rules
⚖️
Classical monetary medium
Gold, silver, wheat, barley, dates, salt or any item that genuinely meets the seven Ribawi characteristics
Gold Dinar, silver Dirham, physical commodity, gold-backed instruments
Classical rules on fiat
🏛️
Fiat — treated as classical money
Mainstream scholarly position: fiat currency carries Thamaniyyah (monetary nature) and is subject to the same rules as gold and silver
Dollar, Pound, Euro — applying full Ribawi conditions as the traditional majority position
→ Routes to classical rules track
Open question
🔬
Fiat - research framework
Research position: fiat fails all seven Ribawi characteristics and may require a different analytical framework from classical monetary media
Fiat-denominated finance where the status of interest on fiat is examined as an open scholarly question
